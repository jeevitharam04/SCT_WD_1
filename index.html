About Buttons

Founded in 2022, Buttons was born out of a passion for creating clothing that blends modern trends with timeless elegance. Our mission is to provide high-quality, sustainably made pieces that empower individuals to express their unique style. Each collection is crafted with attention to detail, from sourcing eco-friendly fabrics to ensuring each design is as comfortable as it is stylish.
